boAt is a company that specializes in affordable, durable, and fashionable audio and wearable products. They refer to their customers as 'boAtheads' and aim to enhance the lifestyle of free-spirited Indians. The company has achieved success and recognition, ranking first in India for truly wireless and earwear, and third globally for earwear and smartwatches. They offer a wide range of products, including wireless earphones, headphones, smartwatches, and home audio. The official website provides information about the company's mission, achievements, and various product categories. They also offer a variety of chargers, cables, and headphones with different features. The company is called Imagine Marketing Limited and is located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.
