1. Overview and Business of the Company

1.1 Business of the company

  • SK Finance Limited is a finance company with a differentiated approach specializing in facilitating financial inclusion for the unserved and underserved sections of society. (Source: Annual_Report_21_22_0df1be1962-pdf.txt)
  • Known for a customer-first and personalized approach with over 29 years of experience as a trusted NBFC in India. (Source: https:__www.skfin.in_about_board-of-directors.txt)
  • Business segments include funding for used and new commercial vehicles, tractors, two-wheelers, and MSMEs. (Source: Annual_Report_21_22_0df1be1962-pdf.txt)
  • Products offered include Commercial Vehicle Loan, Car Loan, Tractor Loan, Construction Equipment Loan, Two-wheeler Loan, Secured Business Loan, and Home Renovation - Mortgage Loan. (Source: https:__www.skfin.in_about_board-of-directors.txt)
