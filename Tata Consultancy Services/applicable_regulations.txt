1. **Corporate Sustainability**
   - Companies Act, 2013, Section 135: Mandates Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) spending and reporting requirements.
   - Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements (LODR) Regulations, 2015: Requires sustainability reporting for listed companies.
   - Business Responsibility and Sustainability Report (BRSR): Reporting framework for sustainability performance.

2. **Financial Reporting**
   - Companies Act, 2013: Governs financial reporting requirements, including preparation and presentation of financial statements.
   - Indian Accounting Standards (Ind AS): Framework for recognition, measurement, presentation, and disclosure of financial information.
   - Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) Regulations: Requirements for quarterly and annual financial reports for listed companies.

3. **Cybersecurity**
   - Information Technology Act, 2000 (IT Act): Provides legal recognition for transactions carried out by means of electronic data interchange and other means of electronic communication.
   - Information Technology (Amendment) Act, 2008: Addresses issues related to cybercrime and electronic commerce.
   - Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011: Prescribes reasonable security practices and procedures for handling sensitive personal data.
   - Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023: Protects personal data and regulates data fiduciaries.

4. **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**
   - Revised AI Advisory by Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY): Emphasizes ethical and responsible AI practices, data protection, privacy, transparency, and accountability.

5. **Cloud Computing**
   - Information Technology Act, 2000: Applies to cloud computing services.
   - Integrated Goods and Services Tax (IGST) Act, 2017: Governs taxation of cloud services.
   - Personal Data Protection Bill, 2018: Provides guidelines for data protection in cloud services.
   - Sector-specific regulations: Banking and insurance sectors have specific guidelines for cloud computing.

6. **Data Analytics**
   - Personal Data Protection (PDP) Bill: Regulates the processing of personal data.
   - Data Protection and Privacy Authority: Enforces data protection regulations.
   - Key Principles: Transparency, consent, lawful basis for processing, data minimization, proportionality, retention, and accountability.

7. **Diversity and Inclusion**
   - Indian Constitution: Forbids discrimination based on gender, disability, caste, and LGBTQ identity.
   - Anti-discrimination laws: Recognize the need for equity and inclusion in the workplace.
   - Upcoming labor codes: Emphasize non-discrimination and equal treatment.

8. **Stock Exchange Listings**
   - Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements (LODR) Regulations, 2015: Governs listing requirements, continuous disclosure obligations, and corporate governance for listed companies.
   - National Stock Exchange (NSE) and Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) regulations: Specific requirements for IT companies listed on these exchanges.
