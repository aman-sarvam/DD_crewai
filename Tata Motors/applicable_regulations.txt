Applicable Indian Regulations for Tata Motors:

1. Corporate Governance:
- SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015: These regulations mandate listed entities to adhere to corporate governance norms, including board composition, audit committees, and disclosure requirements.

2. Financial Reporting:
- SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015: These regulations include comprehensive guidelines on financial disclosures and reporting for listed companies.
- National Financial Reporting Authority (NFRA) Guidelines: The NFRA oversees the compliance of accounting and auditing standards for public and private companies.
- Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) Guidelines: The MCA provides directives on financial reporting, including the preparation and submission of financial statements.

3. Environmental Responsibility and Sustainability:
- Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) Guidelines on Business Responsibility Reporting (BRR): Companies are required to disclose their sustainability initiatives and performance.
- ESG Guidelines for Corporations: These guidelines emphasize the need for companies to adopt sustainable practices, focus on environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors, and report their ESG performance.
